About


IEEE UVCE has come to be perhaps one of the most recognized student branches across the Region 10 of IEEE. Rekindled in 2001, the student branch has since grown to strength of over 200 student members every year.

IEEE UVCE has been interminably striving to hoist the student community and for the retention of its members. The organization’s commitment to enhancing and upgrading the core knowledge base of its members to the industry standards is reflected in the host of activities organized by it throughout the year.

Two events however stand head and shoulders above the sea of activities. ‘Kagada’ the Annual Technical Paper Presentation which has bagged ‘The Darrel Chong Silver Level’ award in the year 2016, organised aims to inspire undergraduate students to take up research and promote critical thinking. ‘Impetus’ the National Level Technical Extravaganza is conducted with a trending theme, as a part of which, a number of workshops are organised. Along with these, ample amount of workshops, symposiums, guest lectures and Industrial visits are organised.

The already impressive collection of activities is further blown out of proportions by the various opportunities provided to its members to serve as a volunteer as well as organise events at the college and section level, such as SYW Congress, WIE symposium, AISYC and other major IEEE events. To encourage women in engineering plentiful events are organised and the female students are given an opportunity to volunteer and participate, among which the prestigious Grace Hopper celebration in computing (GHC), the WIE symposium are worth naming.

IEEE UVCE offers support and encouragement to its members by conducting seminars for free and also reduction in the paid hands on workshop fee. The industrial visits organised provides students a sneak peek into the inner workings of the industries and the thrill of exploration. IEEE UVCE hunts for new talents in college by these events also helping the members develop their organising, leadership and volunteering skills.

Namma UVCE


University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) was established in 1917, by Bharat Ratna Sir M. Visvesvaraya. It is the fifth engineering college to be established in the country, imparting technical education leading to B.E, B.Arch, M.E, M.Arch and PhD degrees in the various disciplines of Engineering and Architecture.

The college is approved by the AICTE and the Government of Karnataka. The departments are accredited with five A+ for three years by the National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi. UVCE has a NAAC rating of five stars for the past four consecutive years.


SIGs


Avishkar
Avishkar is the research club of IEEE UVCE encouraging students to pursue research and projects. Avishkar club has it's flagship event THE PROJECT RUN which is a semester long competition for students to do projects and research. So if you're interested in technical activities, you know where to go!
TISP-STAR
TISP (Teacher In Service Program) and STAR (Student And Teacher Research Program) aim at the improvement in delivery of knowledge . By conducting various workshops for teachers as well as students, these two sigs work to improve the teaching methods adopted and also foster a better bond between students and teachers.
Web Team
Fellas, this is the web tweaking pavillion of SIG. We specialize in conducting all the web related competitions for IEEE UVCE. If Web interests you, then you are welcome to hack your way in. _/\_
Design Team
"A picture speaks a thousand words". It's the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. The form it takes can be physical or virtual. Thus the Designs are created using graphics, words or images and Design Team in IEEE UVCE is taking care about all the designing works needed.
Robotics
Robotics SIG aims to inspire students to design and build manual and auto bots by regularly conducting hands-on workshops and events to showcase the working of bots built by students themselves.
Vidyuth
Wanna know how any of your electronic devices are sculpted and scripted? Is core industry really worth the score? Let's debug, decode and make ourselves technically electrifying!!
Varchas
This is the astronomy club of IEEE UVCE which deals with concepts of science and astro physics. We conduct lot of science fun filled events and people driven by the curiosity of science are most welcomed.
Art Form
Art Forum is an SIG started in 2011 dedicated entirely to artists. Digital art, craft making, decoupage and upcycling are also supported. We strive to bring out the artistic potential of people at UVCE and serve as a platform for them to realise and nurture their talents. We conduct themed contests to encourage artists to present their best work.
